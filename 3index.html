Recipe 1: Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1.5 cups
Baking Powder 3.5 teaspoons
Salt 1 teaspoon
White Sugar 1 tablespoon
Milk 1.25 cups
Egg 1
Butter 3 tablespoons

Step 1: In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar.
Step 2: Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg and melted butter
Step 3: Mix until smooth.
Step 4: Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat.
Step5: Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.
Step 6: Brown on both sides and serve hot.






Recipe 2: Pizza

  • Time: 45 minutes
Ingredients Quantity
Pizza Crust 12 inches
Barbeque Sauce 1 cup
Boneless chicken 2 pieces
Jalapeno peppers (according to taste)
Chopped onions (sccording to taste)
Mozarella cheese 2 cups

Step 1: Preheat oven to 175 degrees C.
Step 2: Place pizza crust on a medium-sized baking sheet. Spread the crust with barbeque sauce. Top with chicken, jalapeno peppers, onions, and cheese.
Step 3: Bake in the preheated oven for 15 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.






Recipe 3: Chicken Burger

  • Time: 80 minutes
Ingredients Quantity
Bonesless chicken *2
Soy sauce 0.25 cups
Burger buns *2
Softened butter 1 tablespoon
Teriyaki Sauce 0.25 cup
Cheese 2 slices
Mayonaise 2 tablespoons
Tomato 2 slices
Iceburg lettuce 2 slices
Other vegetables (according to taste)

Step 1: Place the pieces of chicken into a plastic zipper bag with th esoy sauce, seal the bag, and marinate in the refrigerator for 30 minutes.
Step 2: Spread butter on the cut sides of the burger buns.
Step 3: Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat, and lightly oil the grate.
Step 4: Remove the chicken from the osy sauce, and place the chicken onto the preheated grill, and grill until the chicken shows good grill marks.
Step 5: When the chicken has been almost grilled, brush each piece on both sides with teriyaki to finish grilling.
Step 6: Place a slice of cheese on each chicken piece.
Step7: While chicken is grilling, spread the buns open on the grill and cook until toasted (2 mins).Then, set the buns aside.
Step 8: To assemble, spread the grilled sides of each bun with mayonaise, and top each bottom bun with a cooked chicken piece, a slice of a lettuce, a lice of tomato, and the top bun.
